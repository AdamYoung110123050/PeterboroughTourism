You're not lost, you're home.

A thriving theatre scene and city centre, one of the busiest arenas in the east of England, adventures for time travelling adventurers and even tranquil parks means Peterborough's a great day out!

Jump back to the Bronze Age at Flag Fen, take in the splendour of our Norman cathderal and the sixteenth-century Burghley House, or enjoy one of the most extensive outdoor sculpture collections in the country - which even stretches to our city centre!

We're a thriving metropolitan city with a fantastic culinary scene, lively theatre offer, and huge array of entertainment for all ages. We're proudly home to Posh (Peterborough United) and the Phantoms, as well as competitive rowers and equestrians.

You'll find award-winning green spaces, parks, and beautiful nature reserves less than 10 minutes from our bustling centre where you can find a huge variety of well-known eateries and amazing independents.

We look forward to welcoming you!
